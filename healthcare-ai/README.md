# Healthcare AI

This section covers practical healthcare AI learning with a focus on usefulness, privacy, safety, and human review.

## Important Note

Healthcare AI should support clinicians, researchers, administrators, and patients. It should not replace qualified medical judgment. High-risk outputs must be reviewed by appropriate professionals.

## Topics Covered

- AI for clinicians
- Healthcare research workflows
- Healthcare administration automation
- Clinical documentation support
- Patient education drafts
- Privacy and compliance awareness
- Human-in-the-loop review
- AI risk management in healthcare

## Beginner-Friendly Use Cases

- Summarizing medical research papers
- Drafting patient-friendly education text
- Creating appointment preparation checklists
- Organizing clinical notes for review
- Building administrative automation workflows
- Tracking healthcare AI tools and trends
