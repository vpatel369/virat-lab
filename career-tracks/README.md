# Career Tracks

Virat Lab career tracks translate learning into job-ready skill evidence.

Salary and job-market notes should be refreshed regularly. As of the latest BLS Occupational Outlook data available during this update, information security analysts had a May 2024 median annual wage of $124,910 and projected 2024-2034 employment growth of 29%, while software developers had a May 2024 median annual wage of $133,080 and projected 2024-2034 growth of 15%.

Sources:

- [BLS Information Security Analysts](https://www.bls.gov/ooh/computer-and-information-technology/information-security-analysts.htm)
- [BLS Software Developers](https://www.bls.gov/ooh/computer-and-information-technology/software-developers.htm)
- [BLS Medical and Health Services Managers](https://www.bls.gov/ooh/management/medical-and-health-services-managers.htm)
