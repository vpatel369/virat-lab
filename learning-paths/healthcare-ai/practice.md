# Practice

## Exercises

| Exercise | Goal |
| --- | --- |
| Research summary | Summarize a healthcare AI article |
| Patient education draft | Translate complex text into plain language |
| Privacy review | Identify PHI and sensitive data |
| Workflow risk review | Add review gates and escalation paths |
| Bias check | Ask what populations may be underrepresented |

## Safe Practice Rules

- Use synthetic or public sample data.
- Do not paste real patient data into unapproved tools.
- Mark outputs as drafts.
- Require qualified review for clinical content.

## Progress Checklist

- [ ] I can identify PHI risk.
- [ ] I can write patient-friendly text.
- [ ] I can design human review into a workflow.
- [ ] I can explain AI limitations in healthcare.
