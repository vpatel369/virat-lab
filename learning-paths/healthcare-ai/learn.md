# Learn

## Concepts

- PHI: protected health information.
- PII: personally identifiable information.
- De-identification: removing details that identify a person.
- Clinical decision support: tools that assist, not replace, professionals.
- Human review: required for high-impact health-related outputs.
- Bias: unequal performance or impact across patient groups.
