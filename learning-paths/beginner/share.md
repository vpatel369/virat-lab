# Share

## GitHub Portfolio Guidance

Your project should show how you think, not just what AI generated.

Include:

- Problem statement
- Who the project helps
- Prompt examples
- Safety notes
- Screenshots or sample outputs
- Lessons learned

## LinkedIn Post Template

```text
I just completed my first AI learning project.

I built: [project name]
It helps: [audience]
I practiced: prompt engineering, review, documentation, and safe AI use
Key lesson: [lesson]
GitHub: [link]
```
