# Learn

## Concepts

- AI: software that performs tasks requiring reasoning, perception, language, or decision support.
- LLM: a model trained to understand and generate language.
- Prompt: the instruction and context you give the model.
- Hallucination: output that sounds confident but may be wrong.
- Workflow: a repeatable process with inputs, steps, outputs, and review.
