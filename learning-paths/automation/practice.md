## Hands-On Lab

Design a support ticket triage workflow:

1. New ticket arrives.
2. AI summarizes the issue.
3. AI classifies urgency.
4. Human reviews priority.
5. Ticket is routed.
6. Logs are saved.

## Checklist

- [ ] Uses sample or safe data
- [ ] Documents every input and output
- [ ] Includes human review
- [ ] Stores secrets safely
- [ ] Logs failures
