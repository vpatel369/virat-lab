# Research Workflows

## Weekly AI Research Workflow

1. Pick a theme.
2. Collect 5-10 sources.
3. Prioritize primary sources.
4. Summarize each source.
5. Extract practical lessons.
6. Identify risks and limitations.
7. Save a weekly digest.

## Paper Review Workflow

1. Read abstract and conclusion first.
2. Identify the problem.
3. Identify the method.
4. Identify the dataset or evidence.
5. Identify claims.
6. Identify limitations.
7. Translate the paper into practical lessons.

## Tool Evaluation Workflow

1. Define the use case.
2. Review features.
3. Check pricing and limitations.
4. Review privacy and security posture.
5. Test with sample data.
6. Document recommendation.
